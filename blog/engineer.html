Engineer

2026 · Poem

I am an Engineer.

Not just a builder.
I also picture what to build.
Not just a dreamer.
I also create what is dreamt.

Not just an artist.
My art has no canvas.
Not just a scientist.
My lab has no boundaries.

Not just an entrepreneur.
I create his chance.
Not just a visionary.
I make his world.

More than a professor.
He just teaches what is known.
More than a philosopher.
I turn his thoughts into stone.

They all ask why.
I also ask, “Why not?”
They all ask what.
I also ask, “What if?”

I find a way
When there is none.
I do not rest
Until the dream is done.

Future is imagined by many.
But engineered by few.
And I am that Engineer.
